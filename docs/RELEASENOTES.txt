RELEASE NOTES for berry v.2.0.0

New in 2.0 version:

This version has many changes relative to previous ones.
Therefore, the input and output of most programs may not be compatible with older versions.

The main differences in this version are:

- Improvements of a AI algorithm for the selection of the bands.

- Extension of the software to one and three dimension materials.

- Extension of the software to noncolinear calculations of Quantum ESPRESSO.
